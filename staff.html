Hello!

Friendly, down to earth, and professional are just a few ways to describe our remarkable team here at Urban Waxx. Shannon Conley
Founder

Shannon is the founder and CEO of Urban Waxx. A New Jersey girl at heart, Shannon moved to Portland (by way of Charleston, SC and LA), in January 2007. After 10 years of working in spas and salons all around the country, she opened her dream business: a wax only salon with an over the top customer service philosophy. Shannon is also the creator and developer of Serious Serum.

Do you want to chat her up? Give her some feedback? Ask her a question? Email her: Shannon@urbanwaxx.com
