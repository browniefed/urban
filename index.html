After years of frustration trying to treat post waxxing bimps and rashes, Urban Waxx founder Shannon Conley developed her own solution to soothe and alleviate ingrown hairs for her waxxing clients. Serious Serum. The only all-natural ingrown hair eliminator and skin exfoliant on the market, Serious Serum combines the proven power of AHAs and BHAs with nature’s top skin soothers like green tea, aloe vera and chamomile. It works by encouraging faster cell turnover for softer, smoother skin. It’s so effective in clearing breakouts and minimizing redness that clients love to use it everywhere—fine lines, dark spots, pimples, bumpy, rough skin on backs of arms, and even beard bumps from shaving. It's a cult favorite!
